◆ types_

std::map<std::uintptr_t, std::vector<std::type_index> > tvm::graph::internal::Log::types_

Maps a data address to all the type info associated with this address.

For a given address, each type appears only once, and types are sorted in their order of appearance in the logging process. We assume that the last one to appear is the most derived in the inheritance hierarchy.
