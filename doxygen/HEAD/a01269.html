Detailed Description

This class represents the scalar weight alpha of a constraint, within its priority level.

It is meant to ajust the influence of several constraints at the same level.

Given a scalar weight alpha, and a constraint violation measurement f(x), the product alpha*f(x) will be minimized.

By default the weight is 1.
